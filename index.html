Frequently Asked Questions

Store Ai is a multi-functional and decentralized e-commerce platform that allows the usage of cryptocurrencies to shop online. Other than shopping online, Store Ai allows users to trade and swap cryptocurrencies they may also spend on the online retail store.

Store Ai’s retail store (shop.storeai.org) accepts only Bitcoin (BTC) and Binance Coin (BNB) at the moment. Users could use other cryptocurrencies in the future.

Store Ai’s development team plans to launch a unique exchange and utility token, which users will use for “shopping and exchange” purposes on Storeai.org and its ecosystem. However, the token is yet to be launched or implemented on the platform at the time of writing.
